In this class, students will acquire communication skills necessary for their practice as artists through talking about their own artwork and the work of others. As an attempt to broaden mindsets, the Conceptual Framework will be used as a means to cultivate a critical eye and analyze works of art from multifaceted viewpoints. In workshops and presentations, students will use English (speaking, listening, writing and thinking) in front of others. Talks by guest artists (artists and professionals working in the field of contemporary art) will offer students opportunities to learn about the situation of art overseas through the guests’ own experiences, encounter various creative approaches and ways of thinking, and broaden outlooks as artists who live in modern times without dependence solely on a Japanese context.        <tr bgcolor="#FFFFFF">          <td nowrap bgcolor="#99FFCC">                    			                        <font color="#000000">授業方法</font>			                    </td>          <td colspan="3">                                                  ネイティブ講師による授業（英語でのゼミ形式。教材は英語のみ）<BR/>●	講師によるワークショップ／講義<BR/>［目的］<BR/>・	美術に関連する実践的な英語に慣れ（聞く、喋る）、人前で英語を使うことに自信を持つ。<BR/>・	クリエイティブな行為を通して、自分の意図をうまく伝えられるようにする。<BR/>●	学生によるプレゼンテーション（履修人数によってはグループ・プレゼンテーション）<BR/>［目的］<BR/>・	人前で英語を使うことに自信を持つ。<BR/>・	英語でのプレゼンテーションの力、発言力を養う。<BR/>・	他者に発表する際に自分の意図をうまく伝えられるようにする。<BR/><BR/>日本人講師による授業（日本語でのゼミ形式。教材は英語のみ）<BR/>●	作品分析/批評入門とディスカッション<BR/>［目的］<BR/>・	アーティストとして、自身や作品を取り巻く様々な文脈を理解・意識する。<BR/>・	作品の持つ意味を客観的に分析する力を養う。<BR/>・	ディスカッションを通じて自分の意見を他者と共有する。<BR/><BR/>●	ゲストによるプレゼンテーション（年間約10名の国内外の美術関係者および現代アートの領域で活躍するアーティストを招聘）<BR/>［目的］<BR/>・	国際的な活動経験を持つアーティストや美術関係者をゲストに迎えて、海外の動向、アートに対する姿勢を聞く。<BR/>・	積極的な質疑応答を行い、能動的に英語を使うことに慣れる。<BR/><BR/>【Teaching Method】<BR/>Classes by the native English instructor: (classes are basically taught in English)<BR/>●	Workshops by the native English instructor<BR/>[Objectives]<BR/>・	To develop confidence in using English in front of others after becoming accustomed to the practical use of English in relation to art (listening and speaking).<BR/>・	To communicate ideas effectively through creative activities.<BR/><BR/>●	Presentations by students (this may take the form of group presentations depending on number of students)<BR/>[Objectives]<BR/>・	To develop confidence in using English in front of others.<BR/>・	To develop English presentation techniques and cultivate effective communication skills.<BR/>・	To convey one’s intentions and ideas effectively when presenting to others. <BR/><BR/>Classes by Japanese instructor (classes are basically taught in Japanese)<BR/>●	Analysis of artwork/Introduction to critique and discussion<BR/>[Objectives]<BR/>・	To gain an awareness of and understanding about the various contexts that surround artists and the production of art as artists of the future. <BR/>・	To develop the ability to analyze the meaning of artworks in an objective way.<BR/>・	To share opinions with others through discussion.<BR/><BR/>●	Presentations by guest lecturers (approximately 10 artists and other professionals from Japan and abroad who are working in the field of contemporary art are invited annually.)<BR/>[Objectives]<BR/>・	To learn about trends and attitudes towards art in other countries through presentations by invited artists and art professionals who have experience of working internationally.<BR/>・	To become accustomed to the active use of English through participation in question and answer sessions following presentations.
