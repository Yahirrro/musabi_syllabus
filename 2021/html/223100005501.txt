If you hold a pencile in front of one of these spots, the line connecting pencile and shadow will indicate where the rays of light that form the sopt come from; they are, of course, sunlight that falls through some opening in the foliage: all we see here and there between the leaves is a blinding ray of light.                                      &nbsp;				             </td>        </tr>
