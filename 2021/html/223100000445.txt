The teacher,Craig Sisman,is a native speaker of British English with a background in language education and moving image production. Craig will help students work in groups to produce their own moving image projects. During the course,students will explore(through practical experimentation)the following twelve elements of cinematic art:<BR/>（1） time; （2） light; （3） form; （4） image; （6） space; （7） movement; （8） language （written and spoken）; （9） performance; （10） narrative; （11） sound; （12） music. Students will explore each element from various points-of-view,including naturalism,formalism and abstraction.                                      &nbsp;				             </td>        </tr>
