The structure of paragraphs and essays and ther important components are taught in small, learnable steps.  Clear, relevant models illustrate each step, and varied practices reinforce each lesson.  The models and practices feature current and general academic topics relevant to students' interests in a rapidly changing world.  The ideal student for this course would be one who has achieved an upper intermediate level of English competence and who enjoys writing and reading.  Please be aware that there is a substantial amount of in-class and out-of-class writing in this course.                                      &nbsp;				             </td>        </tr>                                                                                                                                                        <tr bgcolor="#FFFFFF">          <td nowrap bgcolor="#99FFCC">                    			                        <font color="#000000">授業方法</font>			                    </td>          <td colspan="3">                                                  This course will be conducted on an on-demand online basis.
