TOEFL is a standardized test that measures English ability. TOEFL test scores are frequently required when applying to enter college or university in English-speaking countries. As such, students who plan to register for this course should already have achieved an advanced level of English. The course will provide students ample practice for the TOEFL, thus allowing the students the opportunity to familiarize themselves with the test.                                         &nbsp;				             </td>        </tr>                                                                                                                                                        <tr bgcolor="#FFFFFF">          <td nowrap bgcolor="#99FFCC">                    			                        <font color="#000000">授業方法</font>			                    </td>          <td colspan="3">                                                  This course will be conducted on an on-demand (オンデマンド) online basis. Students will have ample opportunity to communicate with and get feedback from the instructor through Teams.                                        &nbsp;				             </td>        </tr>                                                                                                                                                        <tr bgcolor="#FFFFFF">          <td nowrap bgcolor="#99FFCC">                    			                        <font color="#000000">到達目標</font>			                    </td>          <td colspan="3">                                                  Students will focus on the Writing section of the TOEFL test in this course.
